属性说明类型可选值默认值

kind行政区类型DistrictType-DistrictType['AREA']
fillColor填充颜色string-#fdfd27
fillOpacity填充透明度number-1
strokeColor线条颜色string-#231cf8
viewport自动聚焦地图中心boolean-false
